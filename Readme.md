## ✨ what is hacha? ✨

</div>

> **Hacha** is a tiny pixel cat who sits on your desktop, reacts to your emotions, reminds you to eat and sleep, and chats with you whenever you're lonely. she's powered by **Google Gemini AI** and she knows your name is **kvy** ♡

she is not just a widget. she is your **little companion**.

<br>

---

<div align="center">

## 🎀 features 🎀

</div>

<br>

🐾 &nbsp; **AI Chat** — click hacha to open the chat window. tell her anything. she listens, she reacts, she cares.

🌙 &nbsp; **Emotion Reactions** — hacha reads your mood and changes her expression. she won't just mirror you — she *reacts* to you.

⏰ &nbsp; **Smart Reminders** — she reminds you to eat, sleep, go grocery shopping, and take a bath. at the right times. every day.

💕 &nbsp; **Love Timer** — every 30 minutes she randomly tells you she loves you. just because.

🖱️ &nbsp; **Draggable** — pick her up and put her anywhere on your screen. she goes where you go.

🪟 &nbsp; **Always on Top** — she's always visible. always there. you are never alone.

✨ &nbsp; **Transparent Overlay** — magenta background disappears, leaving just hacha floating on your desktop like magic.

## 🚀 getting started 🚀

</div>

### option 1 — just run the exe ✨
```
1. download Hacha.exe
2. double click
3. she appears ♡
4. click her to chat
5. that's it. she lives here now.
```

### option 2 — run from source 🛠️

```bash
# clone the repo
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/hacha.git
cd hacha

# install dependencies
pip install pygame google-generativeai pywin32 

# run!!
python main.py
```

> ⚠️ **windows only** — transparency uses win32 APIs. she won't float on mac or linux (yet).

## 🛡️ antivirus warning 🛡️

</div>

> your antivirus might flag `Hacha.exe` as `Win64:Malware-gen`

this is a **false positive** — super common with PyInstaller apps. hacha is just a cat. she doesn't do anything suspicious. she just loves you.

**to fix it:**
- windows smartscreen → **more info** → **run anyway**
- avast quarantine → **restore** → **add to exclusions**
- windows defender → **allow on device**

she's safe. she's just a cat. 🐾
